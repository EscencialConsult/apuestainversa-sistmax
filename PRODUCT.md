## Brand Personality

Institutional authority, transparent by design, precision-engineered. Three words: rigorous, trustworthy, modern.

## Anti-references

- SaaS-cream / warm-editorial aesthetics (Notion, Linear, Framer)
- Purple-blue gradient dashboards (generic AI SaaS output)
- Playful / startup-friendly UX (Airtable, Webflow)
- Glassmorphism decoratively applied to every surface
- Gradient text on headings

## Design Principles

1. Transparency is the product: every action, amount, and actor is visible to the right role at the right time â€” not hidden behind abstractions.
2. Institutional weight: the interface projects official government infrastructure, not a startup tool. High-stakes decisions require confident, unambiguous affordances.
3. The system disappears into the task: operators manage procurement, not the UI. Familiarity and consistency reduce cognitive load under time pressure.
4. Precision over decoration: data accuracy and legibility outrank visual novelty.
5. Trust through consistency: identical affordances screen-to-screen signal a reliable, well-engineered platform.
